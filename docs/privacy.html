Privacy

Ambient Album Privacy Policy

Photo Library

Ambient Album uses photo library access to display the albums and photos you choose in the app. Your photos stay on your device and are not uploaded by Ambient Album.

Location and Weather

If you enable weather or location-related overlays, Ambient Album may use device location or photo location metadata to show contextual information on screen.

App Settings

Slideshow, overlay, weather unit, display, and album preferences are stored locally on your device so the frame opens with your preferred configuration.

Data Sharing

Ambient Album does not collect or sell personal information. The app is designed around local photo display and local preference storage.
