# Ambient Album

Ambient Album turns your iPhone or iPad into a clean, full-screen digital photo frame for your own photo library.

Use it to show All Photos or selected albums, add a clock and weather overlay, display optional photo details, and keep the device awake for long-running frame sessions.

Ambient Albume is specifically designed for older iPhone and iPad devices, and is compatible with all iOS devices running iOS 17 or newer.

## Highlights

- Full-screen photo slideshow for iPhone and iPad
- All Photos, My Albums, Shared Albums, and Smart Albums support
- Newest First, Oldest First, and Random ordering
- Ken Burns, Dissolve, and Fade to Black transitions
- Crop, letterbox, and blurred-background display modes
- Optional side-by-side portrait photos on landscape displays
- Clock, date, weather, and current device location overlays
- Optional file name, album, and photo location metadata
- Auto-lock prevention for dedicated frame use
- Auto-dim with tap or motion wake
- Overlay drift to reduce static text burn-in risk during long sessions

## Getting Started

1. Open Ambient Album.
2. Allow photo access.
3. Tap the gear button to open **Settings**.
4. Choose albums, order, slideshow timing, fit mode, and overlays.
5. Tap **Done** and leave the app open as a photo frame.

The gear button hides automatically. Tap the screen to show it again.

## Best For

- A bedside or desk photo frame
- A kitchen or living-room iPad display
- Showing family albums, travel photos, or shared albums
- Reusing an older iPhone or iPad as an ambient display

## Permissions

Ambient Album asks for permission only when a feature needs it:

- **Photos** - required to display your slideshow.
- **Location** - used for weather and current device location.
- **Camera** - used for motion wake while the display is dimmed.
- **Network** - used to retrieve current weather.

If photo access is limited, Ambient Album displays only the photos you have allowed.
